# Ohio Water Well Data — County Statistics

County-level summary statistics for documented water wells in Ohio, computed from the
official ODNR well database: median drilled depth, static water level, tested yield,
and modeled drilling cost per county. See [`oh-water-well-county-statistics.csv`](./oh-water-well-county-statistics.csv).

Compiled and maintained by **[Ohio Well Data](https://ohiowelldata.com)** — free per-address well lookups and
full methodology at [ohiowelldata.com](https://ohiowelldata.com). Licensed CC-BY-4.0. Aggregate statistics only;
individual well records are not included.
